UofASec:Cybersecurity

Bi-Weekly Thursdays 15:30 - 18:30 @ Location TBD on UAlberta Campus

Hello hackers!

Welcome to the cybersecurity club at the University of Alberta where we are interested in all things related to information security. We invite everybody to drop into one of our meetings Thursday 15:30 - 18:00 in the computer science building.

Check back here for updates on projects and events on our calendar!

Meetings

We hold bi-weekly meetings every Thursday from 15:30 - 18:00 where we discuss about the latest trends in the field of information security and work together to teach each other new security techniques. We usually meet in CSC B-43/41 or CSC’s open seating area (meeting times will be announced on discord).

We are also always looking for presentations, so please contact us if you are interested with sharing any of your knowledge!

Projects

Our cybersecurity club is always willing to explore and implement new ideas! Please contact us if you would like to share your project or looking for help in your project.

If you’re looking to get started on knowing more about cybersecurity. We recommend signing up for Immersive Labs using your university e-mail and going through the tutorials.

Join Our Discord

Join us in our discord at https://discord.gg/xtGMXAX where you can learn more information about the meeting place and ask questions.
